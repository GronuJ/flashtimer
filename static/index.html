How to use FlashTimer

This tool simulates the League of Legends chat to help you practice tracking summoner spells quickly and efficiently.

The Gamified Practice Loop:

  1. Wait for a flash notification in the chat window.
  2. Press Enter to open the chat.
  3. Calculate the timer (+5 minutes for Flash) and type it (e.g., mid1400).
  4. Send the message to score points! You must log it within 30 real-time seconds of the flash happening.
  5. Build up your streak for bonus points! If you are too slow, your streak breaks.
  6. Use Cmd/Ctrl + A then C to copy old timers and paste them when new flashes happen to maintain the full list!
